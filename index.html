Welcome to FEAST 2018

The 2018 Workshop on Forming an Ecosystem Around Software Transformation (FEAST 2018) will be held in conjunction with the 25th ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security (CCS) on 19 October 2018. The workshop is geared toward discussion and understanding of several critical topics surrounding software executable transformation for improving the security and efficiency of all software used in security-critical applications. The scope of discussion for this workshop will include topics that may be necessary to fully exploit the power and impact of late-stage software customization efforts as described in the Call for Papers.
